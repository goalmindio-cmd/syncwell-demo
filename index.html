About SyncWell

SyncWell is a comprehensive community-based diabetes management platform that bridges the gap between patients and healthcare resources through strategically placed kiosks in public locations. The app gamifies health monitoring by rewarding users with points for regular glucose testing, which can be redeemed at local partner businesses for healthy meals, fitness activities, and educational experiences, creating a sustainable ecosystem that promotes better diabetes self-management while supporting community health initiatives.
